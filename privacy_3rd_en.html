Null Studio Third Party Information Sharing Checklist

In order to ensure the stable operation of our Games, and to realize in-product payment, device manufacturer message push and other related functions, we need to share your personal information with third parties when you use the corresponding functions. Some functions are implemented by accessing software tool development kits (SDKs) provided by third parties. We will not share your personal information with third parties if you are not using the relevant features.

In order to fully and clearly inform you of our information sharing with third parties, we will show the subjects who may share information with game products, as well as the types of personal information, purpose of use and other information as follows. Below, you can find and view the "Third-Party Information Sharing List" and "Third-Party SDK Directory" for the corresponding game product. You can also view the third party's rules for collecting and using personal information through the third-party website link. For details, please refer to the official instructions published by the third party.

We will urge relevant third parties to collect and use your personal information in accordance with our agreement with you in the "Null Studio Privacy Policy", and take appropriate security technology and management measures to ensure the security of your personal information. We will also conduct strict security monitoring on the access third-party software tool development kits (SDKs) to protect the security of your personal information.
